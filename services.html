Our Focus

We provide efficient engineering, construction, design, material procurement, freight and clearing services for the oil and gas industry. We are  proficient  in supervising operations, using skilled manpower, equipment and tools to fabricate, install, test and commission works.

Our Capacity

SUB-SEA 9 OCEAN SERVICES LIMITED has engaged an array of highly experienced staff with many years of experience from both indigenous and foreign origin. This enables us to deliver cost effective projects bearing in mind quality and specification. We have enough human and material resources, technical and administrative know-how to meet the challenges in the oil and gas, and other related industries. We foster use of new technologies so as to take advantage of innovations in the oil and gas industries and provide a wide range of specialized services to our clients.

Our Mission

The mission of SUB-SEA 9 is to develop on a long term bases of its activities by respecting all positive codes of profession and business ethics, to meet the demands of the Nigerian market, to win new markets, to attain the highest level of quality recognized worldwide, efficiency and profitability, and at the same time to apply the highest standards of occupational and environmental protection.

Our Vision

SUB-SEA 9’s priority is to provide services such as military and commercial airplane parts, fabrication, logistics, procurements, and integrated onshore and offshore drilling, well, oilfield services, with highly experience personal and equipment. Another objective of the company, and the responsibility of each employee, is to continually exceed industry standards for health, safety, environment and quality and to improve efficiency and profitability.
